## Key Features

### 1. Authentication & Authorization
- Secure login (JWT)
- RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)
- Session management
- Account suspension

### 2. Product Management
- Create/update/delete products
- Categories & Subcategories
- Brands & Suppliers
- Stock tracking
- Low stock alerts

### 3. Sales System
- Fast sale registration
- Cancel/return sales
- Transaction history
- Statistics & analytics

### 4. Invoice System
- Automatic invoice creation
- PDF generation
- Warranty tracking
- Status management

### 5. Inventory Management
- Stock entries logging
- Stock adjustments
- Inventory history
- Audit trail

### 6. User Management
- Create/update users
- Role assignment
- Account suspension
- Activity tracking
